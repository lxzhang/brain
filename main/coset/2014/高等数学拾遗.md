高等数学拾遗
============


$$\sin \theta = \frac{2 \tan{\frac{\theta}{2}}}{1 + \tan^2\frac{\theta}{2}}$$




$$
\begin{array}{}
d \log x  |&\sec x + &\tan x | &= \sec x       &dx\\
d          & \sec x  &         &= \sec x\tan x &dx\\
d          &         &\tan x   &= \sec^2 x     &dx
\end{array}
$$
